Soul food cafe

From strawberry shortcake to Saucy ribs - Freshly made every morning.

"You're not just visiting a cafe you're coming home. We want to share authentic soul food to preserve black culinary traditions and create a welcoming space rooted in the south end of Seattle. Our name is the name of the street we grew up on and where my mom fed anyone who was hungry. Her motto was if you see the grill smoke coming out of it and you're hungry — stop by and fill your tummy and your soul."
